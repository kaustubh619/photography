Who am I? About Me

Few Words About Myself Nice to Meet You

My name is Sudip Kumar Saha. My association with camera and photography started at a very tender age when I used to fiddle with my father's precious Agfa Box camera. Cameras always fascinated me, enthralled me and enchanted me... the very science behind any object being captured in a film was something which intrigued me very much. By 16, I had learnt about the basic components of a camera and how it is to be used to get the perfect shot. Seeing my enthusiasm for photography, my aunt gifted me a Vivitar film camera when I was still in school. This camera became my prized possession and I used to shoot anything and everything which caught my fancy. Thus, within no time, I became the family photographer.

I completed my formal studies to become an IT professional. Even though my job made me very busy with most of my time being eaten away by my official work, I still managed to squeeze out some time for photography. To gain a mastery of my passion, I started going through books and articles related to photography and learnt about the various techniques. It was at this juncture, I purchased my first SLR camera. As I gained confidence, I started to venture out in areas of scenic abundance. My inclination was more to capture people from various cultures. Thus, with the passage of time - I became a travel photographer, travelling to remote places, capturing the untold stories of men, their cultures and their lands.

It was during 2014 that I got acquainted with some well-known senior photographers, and my journey towards photography took a serious turn. I was lucky enough to accompany them during one of their photo tours and it was indeed a great learning experience for me. I felt as if I have gone through a complete metamorphosis whereby my outlook towards photography had immensely changed for the better. I had become more confident as a photographer.
